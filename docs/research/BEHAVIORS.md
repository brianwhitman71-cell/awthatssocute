- **Scroll trigger**: at 40px scroll, background changes from white/80 transparent to white/95 with backdrop-blur and bottom border
- **Transition**: `transition-all duration-300`
- **Mobile**: hamburger menu reveals nav list below header

## Hero Section
- **Static layout** — no scroll animations
- Pink crochet texture strip at top (hero-1.jpg, opacity-20)
- Purple texture strip at bottom (hero-3.jpg, opacity-15)
- Blue/pink radial gradient watercolor blobs as full-page background
- Gallery grid of 4 product images (2-col mobile, 4-col desktop)
- Three decorative circular images below main text

## Shop Section
- **Product cards**: hover lifts card (-translate-y-1) and deepens shadow
- **Product images**: hover scales image (scale-105) with 500ms transition
- **Add to Cart button**: optimistic UI — shows "ADDED!" for 2s

## Contact Form

- On submit: shows success state with 🧶 emoji
